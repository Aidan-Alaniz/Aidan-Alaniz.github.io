## Overview

As part of the ASCTE 301/401 engineering curriculum, I designed a trebuchet entirely in Fusion 360 before building it with limited, real-world materials.

## Goals

- Apply physics principles (torque, projectile motion) to a physical build
- Practice iterative CAD design and design-for-manufacture constraints
- Maximize projectile distance within material restrictions

## Tools & Skills Used

- **Fusion 360** — parametric 3D modeling and assembly design
- **Physics** — torque calculations, projectile trajectory analysis
- **Fabrication** — translating CAD drawings into a physical prototype

## What I Learned

Balancing the counterweight-to-sling ratio was the most challenging part. Small changes in arm length had a large effect on launch angle. I learned to iterate quickly in CAD before committing materials to a physical build.
